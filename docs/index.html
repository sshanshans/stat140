STAT 140: Introduction to the Ideas and Applications of Statistics

This course introduces you to the discipline of statistics as a science of understanding and analyzing data. Throughout the module, you will learn how to effectively make use of data in the face of uncertainty: how to collect data, how to analyze data, and how to use data to make inferences and conclusions about real world phenomena. Class Format
